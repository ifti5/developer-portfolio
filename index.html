Patenga Sea Beach (পতেঙ্গা সমুদ্র সৈকত) is one of the most beautiful and popular beaches of Bangladesh. Located just 14 km away from Chittagong, it is easy to reach and the popularity of tourists is increasing day by day. In addition, there are also several plans that are being implemented to make the beach more attractive. Now to build a modern and world-class tourist center on the 5 km length Patenga beach is underway. The spectacular decoration has already caught the eyes of everyone.
